Our Story

A Journey of Faith & Purpose

From a humble paddle to a trusted manufacturing partner — discover the heart behind A.M. Rieta Corporation.

Section 1 — Origin

Every Great Journey Begins with a Single Paddle Stroke.

Established in 1999, A.M. Rieta's story is a journey of faith, perseverance, and purpose. Our founder and President, Mr. Alvin M. Rieta, started with a paddle that represents our humble beginnings.

"I am grateful for all the pulling and stretching I've experienced. Despite the pain, paddling helped me find and hone my purpose."
— Mr. Alvin M. Rieta, Founder & President

From a paddle yesterday to machines and equipment today, truly A.M. Rieta has grown and continues to grow. It may not be a perfect company, but it is the right one.


Every great journey begins with a single paddle stroke. Just as our President, Mr. Alvin Rieta, held onto his paddle during humble beginnings —with faith as his anchor and purpose as his guide—together, let’s paddle forward and bring your brand to life.

Our Vision

As we celebrate over 25 years of grace and perseverance, we envision a future where A.M. Rieta Corporation continues to empower brands with cutting-edge formulations, ethical sourcing, and ISO 22716 GMP-compliant manufacturing.

Our Mission

Customer Focus: To support brands with flexible and customized manufacturing solutions tailored to their unique requirements.

Compliance & Safety: To uphold ISO 22716 GMP, FDA, and international regulations for product safety at every stage of production.
