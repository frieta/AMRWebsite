Laboratory Facilities

Advanced Laboratory Facilities

Our laboratory infrastructure is purpose-built to support rigorous testing protocols, ensuring every product released meets our strict quality benchmarks.

🔬
Microbiology

Micro Lab Facility

An isolated room crucial for infection prevention and control. This dedicated facility is used for comprehensive microbial analysis to ensure every product is safe from biological contamination before release.

Products undergo a thorough 3–5 day testing cycle prior to release, covering all required microbiological parameters.

Microbial Analysis (BAM methodology)
Environmental Swab Testing
Gram Staining procedures
Annually calibrated Incubators
Annually calibrated Autoclaves
Biosafety Cabinets (BSC)
⚗️
Physicochemical

Physicochemical & Organoleptic Laboratory

A well-ventilated, safe workspace where trained analysts perform comprehensive testing across a full range of physical, chemical, and sensory product attributes.

All instruments are calibrated annually to guarantee accurate and reproducible testing results across every production batch.

Appearance & Color Analysis (Organoleptic)
pH Testing & Measurement
Viscosity Determination
Electrical Conductivity Testing
Stability & Compatibility Testing
Annually calibrated instruments throughout
