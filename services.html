The ODM Process

The 7-Step Development Process

Our structured, transparent development cycle ensures your product is formulated correctly, tested rigorously, and delivered market-ready.

1
Discovery Phase

Initial Consultation & Requirement Gathering

We begin by deeply understanding your specific needs, unique requirements, target market, and brand standards. This foundation ensures every subsequent step is aligned with your vision and business goals.

2
R&D Phase

Research and Development (R&D)

Our expert R&D team selects appropriate ingredients and creates prototypes with a strict focus on safety, efficacy, and compliance. We leverage the latest formulation science to create effective, market-differentiated products.

3
Testing Phase

Formulation Testing

Rigorous laboratory testing is conducted, including stability testing, compatibility testing, and efficacy trials. Our in-house micro and physicochemical labs provide accurate, traceable results before any product advances.

4
Compliance Phase

Regulatory Compliance

We ensure all formulations meet relevant local and international regulations, including Philippine FDA requirements and international standards such as ISO 22716 GMP. Full documentation is prepared for any required submissions.

5
QA Phase

Quality Assurance

Additional testing rounds are conducted to verify product effectiveness and quality standards before finalization. Our QA team cross-checks all parameters to confirm the formulation is ready for client review.

6
Collaboration Phase

Client Review and Feedback

We present the formulation for your review and incorporate any necessary adjustments. Your satisfaction and alignment with the final product is our priority — we iterate as needed until you are fully confident.

7
Finalization Phase

Final Validation

Final tests — including consumer trials if required — are conducted to confirm real-world performance. Only once the product meets all specifications does it receive full approval for production scale-up.
